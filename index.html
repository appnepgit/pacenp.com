About Us

Two Decades of Engineering Excellence in Nepal

PACE Consultant (P). Ltd. is a Kathmandu-based engineering consultancy established in 2001, specializing in architectural design and infrastructure supervision. For more than two decades, we have partnered with developers, institutions, and government agencies to deliver technically sound, buildable solutions across Nepal — from urban Kathmandu to remote hill districts.

Our work is guided by Nepal's building codes, seismic design standards, and practical site realities. We combine rigorous engineering with clear documentation and responsive project support, ensuring every assignment meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and regulatory compliance.

  • Licensed Engineering Consultancy
  • Member of Nepal Engineering Council
  • ISO Compliant Work Standards
  • Serving Government & Private Sectors
